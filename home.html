What is vim?
Vim is popular text editor known for it speed and feature richness!
It was made by Bram Moolenaar in 1991.
It is also completely keyboard based.
So we cannot use our mouse for anything in vim without tweaks.
That is why vim will be so uncomfortable to learn at first for most people.
It is also one of its major benefits.
